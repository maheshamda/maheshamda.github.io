About Us

DejaBrew was founded in 2023 with a passion for serving the best coffee in town. Our team of baristas are trained to make the perfect cup of coffee every time. We source our beans from small-batch roasters around the world to ensure the highest quality coffee.

At DejaBrew, we believe in giving back to the community. That's why we donate a portion of our profits to local charities that support education and environmental initiatives.
